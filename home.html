We’re Sharestates. Here’s who we are and what we do.

Sharestates is a pioneer in the field of marketplace real estate lending, the online path to consistent passive income for investors and a fresh source of targeted capital for borrowers.

We offer our investors a secure platform from which to select the exact geography and asset class of properties that fit their portfolio and risk tolerance.

We offer our borrowers a non-bank means of finding the funding they need to complete their projects and help their communities thrive whether they’re developing or renovating, or whether they’re focused on commercial or residential properties.

Our Shareline program is designed to institutionalize your lending process. By joining our program you’ll gain access to a backstop of unlimited table funding capabilities at competitive rates – allowing you to focus on your sales & originations process instead of worrying about capital. You’ll gain access to our proprietary portal where you’ll be able to submit loan applications, manage your borrowers, receive and track term sheets, and most importantly – take your files to the closing table.
